Who we are

Visionary Bridge Pvt Ltd is a cutting-edge human resources consulting firm dedicated to delivering innovative and impactful solutions that connect ideas, people, and success. With a strong emphasis on collaboration, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for individuals and organizations aiming for transformative growth. Our expertise spans various domains, enabling us to effectively bridge the gaps between vision and execution while fostering sustainable development. We prioritize people-centric approaches, ensuring a nurturing and inclusive environment for both our employees and partners.

Services

Workforce Supply and Staffing

Temporary Staffing: Providing temporary employees to meet seasonal or project-based demands. Permanent Staffing: Sourcing and placing full-time employees.
Contract Staffing: Supplying professionals for specific contract-based roles.

Recruitment Services

End-to-end recruitment solutions for junior, mid-level, and senior positions. Specialized recruitment for industries such as IT, healthcare, logistics, etc. Campus recruitment drives and bulk hiring solutions.

Skilled and Unskilled Labor Supply

Providing skilled workers, such as technicians, engineers, or IT professionals. Supplying unskilled or semi-skilled labor for manufacturing, construction, or other sectors.

HR Outsourcing

Managing all or part of a company’s HR functions, such as recruitment, payroll, and compliance. Providing remote or on-site HR professionals to oversee operations.

Managed Staffing Solutions

Acting as a Managed Services Provider (MSP) to oversee the contingent workforce. Providing Vendor Management System (VMS) platforms to manage staffing vendors effectively.

Digital HR Tools and Support

Offering HR technology solutions like Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) or employee portals. Providing time-tracking and task management tools for contingent staff.

Payroll and Compliance Management

Handling payroll for supplied employees, ensuring legal and tax compliance. Ensuring labor laws, minimum wage standards, and statutory benefits are adhered to.

Industry-Specific Workforce Solutions

Providing tailored HR solutions to industries like healthcare, IT, construction, logistics, etc.
